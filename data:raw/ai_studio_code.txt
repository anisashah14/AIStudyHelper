COURSE: ENG 310 - Advanced Technical Communication
PROFESSOR: Dr. Aris Thorne
GRADING SYSTEM: Specifications ("Spec") Grading

This course does not use points, percentages, or averages. Every assignment is graded purely as "Satisfactory" (S) or "Unsatisfactory" (U). To earn a specific letter grade in this course, you must complete the specific "Bundle" of achievements listed below. 

You cannot "average out" a bad grade. If you miss one requirement for the 'B' bundle, you drop to a 'C', regardless of the quality of your other work.

THE GRADE BUNDLES:

To earn an "A" in the course, you must achieve ALL of the following:
- Attend at least 13 of the 15 seminar sessions.
- Earn an "S" on all 4 Core Memos.
- Earn an "S" on the Major White Paper.
- Lead 2 class discussion panels.
- Pass the Final Ethics Audit with zero major infractions.

To earn a "B" in the course, you must achieve ALL of the following:
- Attend at least 12 of the 15 seminar sessions.
- Earn an "S" on at least 3 of the 4 Core Memos.
- Earn an "S" on the Major White Paper.
- Lead 1 class discussion panel.
- Pass the Final Ethics Audit with no more than 1 major infraction.

To earn a "C" in the course, you must achieve ALL of the following:
- Attend at least 10 of the 15 seminar sessions.
- Earn an "S" on at least 2 of the 4 Core Memos.
- Submit the Major White Paper (even if graded 'U', it must be submitted).

Tokens: You are issued 2 "Flex Tokens" at the start of the semester. A token can be used to resubmit a 'U' assignment for a second attempt, or to excuse one absence.
